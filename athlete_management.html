Athlete Management — Build a career, not just a performance.

Khel Sahayog Foundation's Athlete Management program runs parallel to our grassroots work, providing comprehensive career support to talented athletes who have emerged from our ecosystem or are identified through scouting. We don't just develop players—we build sustainable athletic careers.

The Critical Gap: Why Talent Fails

Visualizing the cause and effects in Indian sports

The Causes (The Gaps)

Lack of Infrastructure

Limited access to quality training facilities and equipment

No Professional Pathways

Unclear routes from amateur to professional sports

Financial Barriers

High costs of training, equipment, and competition

Limited Coaching

Shortage of qualified coaches and mentors

The Consequences (The Lost Potential)

Talent Wastage

Gifted athletes never reach their full potential

High Dropout Rates

Athletes abandon sports due to lack of support

Lost Potential

India misses out on world-class athletes

Economic Impact

Loss of sports industry growth and employment

Our Athlete Management Model

We follow an integrated, athlete-first model under six service pillars:

Scouting & Onboarding

Identifying talent through tournaments, grassroots programs, and partnerships. Comprehensive assessment and enrollment process.

Holistic Performance Support

Strength & conditioning, sports science, nutrition, mental conditioning, and injury prevention programs.

Competition & Exposure

Access to national and international tournaments, competitive leagues, and performance tracking systems.

Sponsorship & Commercial Management

Brand partnerships, endorsement deals, contract negotiations, and financial planning for athletes.

Education & Career Transition

Academic support, vocational training, and career planning for life beyond competitive sports.

Legal & Safeguarding

Contract review, rights protection, anti-doping compliance, and athlete welfare safeguards.

Service Tiers
