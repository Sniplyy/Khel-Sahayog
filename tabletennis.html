Table Tennis : Precision, Focus and Fast Reflexes

The Power of Table Tennis

Table Tennis builds essential skills that benefit athletes on and off the field:

Hand-eye coordination
Quick decisions
Mental focus
Agility
Concentration
Balance

Global Context

China, Japan, and South Korea dominate table tennis by combining early exposure with high-quality coaching.

In India

West Bengal and Gujarat have strong Table Tennis cultures with deep club ecosystems.

Focus States

West Bengal, Gujarat

OUR PROGRAMS

Grassroots Programs

  • Dedicated Table Tennis labs in schools equipped with 1–4 professional-grade tables for training
  • Regular monthly ranking matches and competitive circuits to develop tournament experience and skills
  • Comprehensive racket and equipment sponsorship programs ensuring access to quality gear for all players

Athlete Management

  • Advanced technical analysis using video recording and data analytics for performance optimization
  • International exposure tours and competitive opportunities to gain global tournament experience
  • Access to ITTF-level coaching expertise and training with specialized technical development programs
