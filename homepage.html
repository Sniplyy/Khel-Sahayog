EMPOWERING INDIA'S NEXT CHAMPIONS

From Grassroots fields to professional arenas, Khel Sahayog Foundation creates complete sports ecosystems. Nurturing first-time players to elite athletes through grassroots programs, coach training and professional management, transforming potential into performance and livelihoods.

EMPOWERING INDIA'S NEXT CHAMPIONS

End-to-End Pathways

We build complete ecosystems that transition athletes from local village grounds to professional leagues and international arenas.

Professional Career Management

We provide elite support, including sports science, sponsorship negotiation, and legal protection, to turn talent into a stable livelihood.

Grassroots Infrastructure

We establish community training centers and talent ID camps to ensure children from rural backgrounds have access to structured sports early on.

Sport for Social Change

We use athletics as a platform to drive mental health awareness, women's empowerment, and drug prevention in vulnerable communities.

Why this work is needed

With Support

  • Talent is identified and nurtured early.
  • Access to professional equipment and safe play spaces.
  • Clear pathways from passion to professional careers.
  • Mentorship that protects against negative influences.
  • Holistic support for long-term growth and livelihood.

Without Support

  • Talent remains undiscovered.
  • Lack of equipment and safe spaces.
  • No clear path from passion to profession.
  • Vulnerability to negative influences.
  • Limited opportunities for growth.
