Grassroots Development

Grassroots Development - Building India’s Sports Foundation

Who We Are?

Khel Sahayog Foundation operates at the intersection of grassroots talent identification and professional athlete management. We don't just discover talent—we nurture it from the ground up, creating a seamless pathway from dusty village grounds to national stadiums. Our grassroots development programs serve as the foundation, identifying and developing young athletes across India, while our athlete management services ensure that promising talent receives the professional support needed to succeed at the highest levels.

Why Grassroots Matters?

India has immense sporting potential, but most of it remains untapped due to lack of early exposure, structured systems, and quality coaching at the foundational level. Grassroots development is where champions are made—not in elite academies, but in community centers, school playgrounds, and local tournaments. By investing in grassroots sports, we create a robust pipeline of talent, ensure inclusivity, and build a sporting culture that can compete on the world stage.

Grassroots By Sport

Sport-specific development programs across India

Hockey - India's Heritage, India's Future

Khel Sahayog will build hockey centres in Punjab and Odisha with a clear grassroots-to-national pathway.

Kabaddi - India's Indigenous Sport

Khel Sahayog will set up school kabaddi clubs in Haryana and Tamil Nadu for Pro Kabaddi scouting.

Football - Growing the Beautiful Game

Khel Sahayog will operate football academies in key states with pathways to ISL and I-League teams.

Rugby - Leadership & Confidence

Khel Sahayog will run touch rugby programs in NCR and Tamil Nadu leading to the national circuit.

Table Tennis - Precision, Focus & Agility

Khel Sahayog will establish TT labs in West Bengal and Gujarat with pathways to the national circuit.

Athletics - Foundation for All Sports

Khel Sahayog will set up athletics coaching pods in Haryana, Kerala and Odisha leading to pro athletes.

Badminton - Accessible High-Performance Sport

Khel Sahayog will establish rural badminton centres in Telangana, Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka.
