Drug Awareness and prevention

Substance abuse among youth in India has risen sharply. Vulnerable youth – especially those without stable mentorship or community belonging – are at highest risk. Sport is a powerful preventive tool because it offers purpose, community, and identity. Yet without proactive awareness and intervention, young athletes can still drift into substance use.

The Problem and The Gap

The Problem

  • Rising Substance Abuse Among Youth
  • Lack of Awareness About Drug Risks
  • Peer Pressure and Social Influence
  • Limited Access to Preventive Education
  • Inadequate Support Systems for At-Risk Youth

The Gap

  • Few Community-Based Prevention Programs
  • Insufficient Engagement Activities for Youth
  • Limited Role Models and Mentorship
  • Lack of Safe Recreational Spaces
  • Minimal Integration of Sports in Prevention

What We Do (Our Solution)

Sensitization Sessions

Interactive workshops and awareness programs educating youth about the dangers of substance abuse and building resilience against peer pressure.

Sports-Led Engagement

Structured sports programs that provide healthy alternatives, build discipline, and channel youth energy into positive activities and skill development.

Community Partnerships

Collaboration with schools, local organizations, and families to create comprehensive support networks and sustainable prevention ecosystems.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Continuous tracking of program effectiveness, participant progress, and community impact to ensure sustainable outcomes and data-driven improvements.
