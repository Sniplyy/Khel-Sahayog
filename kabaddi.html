Kabaddi builds explosive power, breath control, strategic thinking, and mental toughness.

Why Kabaddi?

The Power of Kabaddi

Kabaddi builds essential skills that benefit athletes on and off the mat:

Explosive Power
Breath Control
Strategic Thinking
Mental Toughness
Cultural Pride

Focus States

Grassroots Programs

  • School Kabaddi Clubs with trained coaches
  • Safe mat infrastructure to reduce injuries
  • Annual and seasonal Kabaddi Leagues
  • Workshops on nutrition, recovery, and injury prevention

Athlete Management

  • Access to strength & conditioning specialists
  • Exposure to PKL scouting and franchise trials
  • Professional contract and negotiation support
  • Injury management and long-term career planning
