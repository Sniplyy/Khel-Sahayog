EMPOWERING INDIA'S NEXT CHAMPIONS

From Grassroots fields to professional arenas, Khel Sahayog Foundation
 creates complete sports ecosystems. Nurturing first-time players to elite athletes through grassroots programs, coach training and professional management, transforming potential into performance and livelihoods.

EMPOWERING INDIA'S NEXT CHAMPIONS

END-TO-END PATHWAYS

We build complete ecosystems that transition athletes from local village grounds to professional leagues and international arenas.

CAREER MANAGEMENT

We provide elite support, including sports science, sponsorship negotiation, and legal protection, to turn talent into a stable livelihood.

GRASSROOTS INFRASTRUCTURE

We build community centers and talent ID camps to ensure rural children have early access to structured sports.

SPORT FOR SOCIAL CHANGE

We use athletics to drive mental health awareness, women's empowerment, and drug prevention in vulnerable areas.

WHY THIS WORK IS NEEDED

With Support

  • Talent identified and nurtured early.
  • Access to equipment and safe spaces.
  • Clear pathways to professional careers.
  • Protection from negative influences.

Without Support

  • Talent remains undiscovered.
  • No equipment or safe spaces.
  • No path to professional careers.
  • Exposure to negative influences.
  • Limited opportunities for growth.
