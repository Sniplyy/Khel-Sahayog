Global Context

New Zealand, South Africa, England, and Australia dominate rugby through decades of investment in youth development, structured leagues, and professional pathways.

In India

Rugby is growing in states like Odisha, West Bengal, and Maharashtra. India has potential in rugby sevens, a faster, more accessible format.

Focus States

We focus on Odisha, West Bengal, and Maharashtra, providing structured training and competitive opportunities.
