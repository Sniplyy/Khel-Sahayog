Rugby — Building Courage, Leadership and Community

The Power of Rugby

Rugby fosters physical strength, endurance, courage, resilience, teamwork, trust, leadership and communication.

Global Context

New Zealand, South Africa, England, and Australia dominate rugby through decades of investment in youth development, structured leagues, and professional pathways.

In India

Rugby is growing in states like Odisha, West Bengal, and Maharashtra. India has potential in rugby sevens, a faster, more accessible format.

Focus States

We focus on Odisha, West Bengal, and Maharashtra, providing structured training and competitive opportunities.

Grassroots Programs

  • Structured touch rugby programs designed for children to learn the game safely and enjoyably
  • Comprehensive coach training programs focused on teaching safe contact techniques and player welfare
  • Engaging mixed-gender rugby festivals promoting inclusivity and community building

Athlete Management

  • Carefully structured gradual progression pathway from non-contact touch rugby to full contact play
  • Comprehensive access to professional-grade safety gear and protective equipment for all players
  • Strategic regional club placements and partnerships
