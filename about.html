ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE

Khel Sahayog Foundation is a sports-driven social impact organisation committed to empowering children, youth, and vulnerable communities across India through sports, education, health, and life-skills development.

By connecting grassroots programs coach development and athlete management, Khel Sahayog builds pathways from village grounds and government schools to state academies, leagues, and professional contracts

Our Vision

To create inclusive, accessible, and high-impact programs around:

  • Physical fitness and health
  • Emotional resilience and mental well-being
  • Life skills like discipline, teamwork, and leadership
  • Pathways to education, employment, and professional sport

so that children and youth from all backgrounds can use sport as a tool for life-long growth and livelihood.

Our Mission

To create inclusive, accessible, and high-impact programs around:

  • Sports training and competitions
  • Talent identification and athlete management
  • Mental health and well-being
  • Women's empowerment and safety
  • Drug awareness and prevention
  • Job skilling and career pathways

Our Values

Inclusivity

We believe talent knows no boundaries. Our programs actively reach rural and underprivileged communities, ensuring equal opportunity for all.

Access

We break down barriers by providing equipment, facilities, and coaching to communities that need it most.

Empowerment

We nurture not just athletes, but complete individuals—focusing on physical, mental, and emotional growth.

Integrity

We operate with transparency and accountability, ensuring every contribution creates measurable impact.

Impact

We build programs designed for long-term impact, creating self-sustaining ecosystems that benefit communities for generations.

The Role of Tournaments

  • Scout Visibility: Professional scouts and talent identifiers attend our tournaments to discover emerging athletes
  • Competitive Exposure: Athletes gain experience competing at progressively higher levels
  • Performance Benchmarking: Tournaments provide objective metrics to track athlete development
  • Community Engagement: Local tournaments build community support and create role models
