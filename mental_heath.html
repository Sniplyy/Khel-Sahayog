Mental Health for Youth

Young athletes in India face unprecedented stress – from family pressure to perform, social media comparisons, identity confusion, and uncertainty about the future. Many show signs of anxiety, depression, and burnout without access to professional mental health support. Sports offer a unique pathway to mental well-being, but only when paired with structured psychological support.

The Problem and The Gap

Why This Matters

  • 1 in 5 youth athletes experience anxiety or depression related to sports performance.
  • Many young athletes face burnout, stress, and identity crises when their self-worth becomes tied solely to their athletic success.
  • Mental health challenges often go unaddressed due to stigma, lack of awareness, or limited access to support.
  • Without proper mental health care, young athletes may struggle with long-term emotional and psychological issues.

The Gap

  • Limited access to mental health professionals who understand the unique pressures faced by young athletes.
  • Lack of peer support networks where athletes can share their struggles without judgment.
  • Insufficient education on mental health, resilience, and coping strategies in sports programs.
  • Cultural stigma around mental health in sports communities, preventing athletes from seeking help.

What We Do (Our Solution)

Professional Counseling

Access to individual and group counseling sessions for athletes to process their emotions safely.

Peer Support Circles

Creating safe, judgment-free spaces where athletes can share experiences and support one another.

Resilience Workshops

Interactive sessions on managing stress, anxiety, body image, and handling competitive pressure.

Performance & Recovery

Mental skills training for high-pressure moments, plus psychological support for injury comebacks.

Join Our Mission

Mental health is not a luxury—it's a necessity. By supporting our Mental Health for Youth initiative, you're helping young athletes build the emotional strength they need to succeed in sports and in life. Together, we can break the stigma, provide critical resources, and create a healthier, more supportive environment for the next generation of athletes.
